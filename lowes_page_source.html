This Frigidaire 18 cu. Ft. top freezer refrigerator offers a timeless style with the latest cooling technology. Our EvenTemp™ Cooling System prevents unwanted freezing by optimizing airflow and ensures a consistent temperature throughout your refrigerator. The spacious interior storage enables you to easily organize your family's favorites with two wire shelves, spacious door bins, and a full-width glass freezer shelf.

  • Reversible doors: Refrigerator and freezer doors are reversible for optimized flexibility

  • Auto-close doors: close if left open 5-in or less

  • Prevent warm spots and unwanted freezing with our EvenTemp cooling system's optimized airflow that ensures a consistent temperature throughout your refrigerator

  • Keep produce fresh for longer with our humidity-controlled crisper drawers

  • Each refrigerator goes through a 42-point quality check to ensure strong performance for years to come; 1 year limited warranty

  • Ice maker ready - enjoy the flexibility of later adding an ice maker without sacrificing valuable freezer space thanks to our optional slim ice maker (sold separately - model # IM117000)

  • Garage Ready- Designed to perform in 45F – 110F conditions to ensure optimal performance in a variety of climates

  • Electronic temperature control; frost-free
